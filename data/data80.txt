"This is a list of adult [[fiction]] books that topped '''[[List of The New York Times Fiction Best Sellers|''The New York Times'' Fiction Best Sellers list]] in 1944.'''\n\nThe most popular books of the year were ''[[A Tree Grows in Brooklyn (novel)|A Tree Grows in Brooklyn]]'', by [[Betty Smith]] and ''[[Strange Fruit (novel)|Strange Fruit]]'', by [[Lillian Smith (author)|Lillian Smith]] with respectively 22 and 15 weeks at the top. A \"[[Sleeper hit|sleeper]]\" success, ''A Tree Grows'' had been published fully 6 months before it eventually made it to the No. 1 spot.
